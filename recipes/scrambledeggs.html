Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Teaspoon Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Pour oil into pan, get it ripping hot.
  2. Hard-scramble the eggs, adding salt during the cooking process
  3. Remove from heat, add any addition spices or chives if you wish.
  4. Enjoy!

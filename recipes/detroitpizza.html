Ingredients

  • 1 Can Storebough dough
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Can of Tomato Sauce
  • 8 oz Brick Cheese
  • 1 Teaspoon Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Open the can of dough, spread it into pan, and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Open the can of sauce, add edge to edge.
  3. Spread the cheese edge to edge as well, making sure to get into corners
  4. Place in 500 degree oven for 20 minutes,remove and eat!

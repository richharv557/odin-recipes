Ingredients

  • 1 Can Chickpeas
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 1/4 Cup Tahini
  • 2 Tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 Teaspoon Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Open can of chickpeas, drain, and add into food processor.
  2. Peel and dice garlic, add into food processor.
  3. Add the tahini, lemon juice, and salt to the food processor, and run it for 60 seconds.
  4. Enjoy!
